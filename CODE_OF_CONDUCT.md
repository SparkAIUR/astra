# Code of Conduct

This project adopts the Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct, version 2.1.

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- Demonstrating empathy and kindness.
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Giving and receiving constructive feedback gracefully.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainers via the private security/contact channel.

For the full text, see https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/
